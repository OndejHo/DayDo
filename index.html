Privacy Policy

Privacy Screen Demo

Last updated: 2026-03-05 Contact: horakondrej@icloud.com

Privacy Screen Demo is a demonstration app that shows how a privacy screen feature could behave. The blur effect applies only to the demo screen inside the app.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. The app does not use analytics or tracking.

Camera Usage

The app may use the device camera only to detect whether a face is present and how many faces are visible, in order to demonstrate privacy behavior (for example, blurring when more than one person is looking).

  • All processing happens locally on your device.
  • No photos or videos are recorded or saved by the app.
  • No biometric identifiers are stored or transmitted.

Photos

If you choose to pick an image, it is used only to display the demo preview. The image is kept in memory for the current session and is not stored by the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
